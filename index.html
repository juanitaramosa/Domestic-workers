COVID-19 LEAVES DOMESTIC WORKERS IN POVERTY

Verónica Ramírez, used to work for 3 families, two of them left the City. She works now one day per week in a penthouse at the Financial District where she gets pay $150 per day. Verónica is currently earning $600 per month now. She haven't be able to pay rent since April and has not recibe any federal or state aid. In order to get to work she has to spend $5.5 in a metrocard and she’s spending almost $20 weekly in gloves, masks, and sanitiazer. She is one of the 2.2 million people in the country who work as domestic workers in private homes.
